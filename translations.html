Translations
Exactitude in Science

I have found that translation can be a useful exercise in the learning of a language and the training of the mind to think other than what it has become accustomed to. The wonder of understanding the thoughts of another person from a time long gone fills one with the sensation of flying.

What follows is a selection of the translations I have made over time. Translation is always a synthetic act, an act of understanding and of placing together, a reading in the true sense of the latin legere.
